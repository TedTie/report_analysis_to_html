# Company and Role Background

This reference provides default context for 51Talk-style sales and operations reports. Use it to understand likely business meaning, not to force conclusions.

## Company Context

51Talk is an online English education business. Reports may involve sales performance, paid conversion, lesson attendance, referral growth, live class operations, parent/student community operations, and cross-team execution.

## Common Roles

- CC: front-end sales/customer consultant role. Often connected to market leads, referral leads, registration, booking, attendance, payment, and early-stage conversion.
- SS: back-end sales/service sales role. Often connected to renewals, existing student value, follow-up, package strategy, pricing, and longer-cycle conversion.
- Operations: role focused on process, funnel, live events, community, content, campaign execution, data tracking, and cross-functional coordination.
- Leader/Group Lead: role responsible for team output, people management, process management, coaching, standards, review cadence, and future planning.

## Usage Rules

- Confirm the user's exact role and local business definitions.
- Do not assume CC/SS responsibilities are identical across regions or teams.
- Use this context to ask better questions and avoid naive interpretation.
- If the user's document defines a term differently, follow the user's document after confirmation.
